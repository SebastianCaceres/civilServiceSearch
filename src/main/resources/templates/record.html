Status & Estimation Caveats

Reachable: Indicates that the candidate’s rank was certified to an agency on a list. This means they were eligible to be considered for hiring, but does not guarantee they were selected.

Appointed Status: Verified on-demand against the NYC Civil List payroll dataset. A candidate is flagged as Appointed if they appear on the city payroll for a calendar year after their list certification year. Payroll data updates annually and may experience lag.

Estimated Days to Reach: Timelines are calculated using a linear regression projection of historical agency request rates for this list. Actual reach times vary based on agency budgets, vacancies, and hiring freezes.
